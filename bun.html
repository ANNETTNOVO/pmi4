ABOUT US

Traditions
Traditional hand-dipping techniques that create truly unique confections.
Philosophy
We change the taste of everyday life, creating new traditions for food, communication and inspiration.
Mission
Our mission is to create high-quality products and develop original and unique quality recipes with complex yet balanced flavours.
MORE ABOUT US
We were interested in how the bakery combines tradition and the spirit of the times to meet today's needs. So it's time for bread. A place where bread gets what it needs to be good: the best ingredients, peace of mind and care. A place where you can watch bakers at work. A place where, like bread, you can take the time to enjoy what has been done for enjoyment.
QUALITY
The most important thing is that the quality is right. All of our products are made exclusively with the best Bioland ingredients. Chemical additives must stay outside. Some of the recipes have been tinkered with for generations. Real people bake. Overall, these things make the difference that you can taste.
